Hello, I'm Arthur, PhD student at the Dept. of Ecology & Evolutionary Biology at UofT.

Research

I'm currently interested in how the physiology of organisms are associated with the distribution and evolution of species. I am fascinated by the diversity of plants on our planet and the processes that led to its current state. I have been working with Dr. Rowan Sage to examine the drivers of evolution of C4 photosynthesis. Currently, I am working on characterizing Tribulus, a lineage of C3 and C4 plants. In a separate project, I am evaluating the performance of C3-C4 intermediates under photorespiratory conditions. I am grateful for support from a Queen Elizabeth II/Charles E. Eckenwalder Scholarship in Science and Technology.
