Research

I am an undergraduate student at Western University. I'm currently interested in the biochemical mechanisms underlying the acclimation of photosynthesis to stress. In September 2019, I joined Dr. Norman P. A. Hüner’s lab to assist with a project conducted at the Biotron Experimental Climate Change Research Centre. The objective of this project is to investigate the effects of climate change on the photosynthesis of several boreal tree species. We are examining fluorescence, protein, and pigment patterns of boreal trees grown in elevated CO2 and temperatures.
